This privacy policy applies to the sci-gen iOS Micro Apps collection.

Data collection

The apps are designed as local-first utilities. They do not require account creation and do not use developer-hosted servers for normal app functions.

Local data

Some apps may store user-created notes, logs, settings, checklists, calculations, photos, or exported files locally on the device. This data remains under the user's control unless the user chooses to share or export it through iOS sharing features.

Device permissions

Some apps may request permissions such as camera, microphone, photo library, location, motion sensors, calendar, or notifications when a feature requires it. Permission use is limited to the feature shown in the app.

Analytics and tracking

The apps do not include third-party advertising SDKs and are not designed to track users across apps or websites.

Support contact

If a user contacts support, the information included in the support message is used to respond to the request and investigate reported issues.

Changes

This policy may be updated when app features or platform requirements change.
