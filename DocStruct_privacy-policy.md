## Privacy Policy for DocStruct

DocStruct is a desktop application for loading PDF files, previewing pages, splitting spreads, and exporting processed PDF files.

This application does not collect, store, or share personal information through the developer's own servers.

All core PDF processing is performed locally on your device.

### Information handled by the app

The application may locally handle the following user-selected data on the device:

- PDF files selected by the user
- temporary preview cache generated for page rendering
- output PDF files exported by the user

### External transmission

As of the current version, DocStruct does not send PDF contents, extracted text, or processing results to the developer's servers.

The application also does not use analytics, telemetry, or advertising SDKs inside the app.

### Third-party services

If this policy page is hosted by GitHub Pages or another hosting provider, access to the page itself may be logged by that hosting provider under its own policies.

### Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated when the app's features or operational policy changes.
